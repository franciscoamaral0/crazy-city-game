CRAZY CITY, THE GAME

About

Project carried out as part of the web development bootcamp held at IRONHACK . The game takes place in a big city, where there are pedestrians intensely crossing the street, our illustrious driver, Mario, will have to dodge, the problem is that there are some citizens who run when crossing. Our Mario will have to be very careful to reach the end of the avenue.
